About NYC Audiology

Dr. Winnie Feng-Gring AuD

Dr. Winnie Feng-Gring has been providing audiological services to the New York City community for nearly 12 years. Helping her patients rediscover the gift of sound is what really makes her day!

“I pride myself on ensuring each patient’s complete satisfaction with the services provided to them. I feel strongly about taking the time to present a range of options to the patient, and doing so in a clear, comprehensive and congenial manner.

My services and approach are tailored to each individual patient’s needs, and I personally follow up on each case to ensure that my patients’ needs and expectations have been met and exceeded.”
