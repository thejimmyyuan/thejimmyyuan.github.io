Corporate Web Design

You're a web developer at a huge corporation, things are going to be different. In the real world compared to school it's just two different animals.

The biggest key skill in corporate web design is the ability to Google. You will need to reverse engineer a lot of effects you see elsewhere and apply them where you see fit.

Things to note in a corporate environment

  • If you need help, don't be afraid to ask
  • Ask for second and even third opinions
  • Most of the time, they will never have the IDE you want
  • Dreamweaver is always a safe bet
  • Make sure what your manager wants
  • Make your code easy to read
  • Related to the previous point, indent properly
  • Cross compatibility matters
