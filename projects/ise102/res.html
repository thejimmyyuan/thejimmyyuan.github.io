Why Responsive Design?

Now a days, since everyone is on their phone, both web apps and website have to be responsive. They have to look good and feel good on multiple devices.

However this is actually really hard, you want to build your website from the smallest device to the way up. And check what kind of website you're making.

There's multiple ways of creating, a responsive design. @Media queries are one of them, and for bigger projects using a framework is also a very good idea.
